Social Media Manager Website That Lands Premium Clients

Client : Eni Michelle

Year: 2025

Social media manager Eni Michelle was struggling to establish her business professionally online. She needed a website that reflected her personality and energy while clearly communicating her value to turn visitors into loyal customers.

My Goal

My goal was to exceed her expectations and bring her vision to life and that’s exactly what I did. A personalized website would clearly communicate who she is, set her business apart, and help turn visitors into paying clients, something essential for growing her business.

Process

  1. Discovery: Discussed the client’s goals, brand vision, and what they wanted their website to achieve.
  2. Planning: Outlined the site structure and features needed for a seamless user experience.
  3. Design Inspiration: Collected references and agreed on a style direction based on the client’s preferences.
  4. Development: Built a clean, responsive, and fast-loading website with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  5. Testing: Checked performance, responsiveness, and browser compatibility before launch.
  6. Delivery: Deployed the site, shared it with the client, and made final tweaks based on feedback.

Obstacles & Solutions

Lack of Initial Design Direction

Obstacle: The client didn’t have a clear idea of what they wanted visually for their site.

Solution: I researched professional websites in her niche and created design mockups she could easily pick from, which helped speed up decision-making.

Minimal Content Provided

Obstacle: There wasn’t much text or imagery ready for the website.

Solution: I drafted compelling copy and sourced high-quality, relevant images that matched her brand, then refined them together with her feedback.

Results

  • Killer Upwork Win: Soon after launching her site, Eni Michelle landed a high-paying Upwork gig — a job she had once been overlooked for.
  • 30% Increase in Conversions: The redesigned website led to significantly more inquiries and sales opportunities within the first two weeks of launch.
  • Improved Engagement: Visitors spent more time on the site thanks to a cleaner layout and faster load times, reducing bounce rates by 40%.
  • Professional Online Presence: The client received positive feedback from her audience and industry peers, enhancing her credibility and brand image.
