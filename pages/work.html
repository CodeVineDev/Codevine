How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple project might take 5-7 days, while more comprehensive websites can take 1-2 weeks. I will provide a specific estimate after our initial consultation.

Can you work with my existing brand and website?

Absolutely! I'm experienced in working with established brands. I will ensure all new updates align perfectly with your existing brand identity and style.

What makes your building process unique?

My process stands out due to our collaborative approach. I involve you at every stage, ensuring the final product truly reflects your vision while benefiting from my expertise.

Do you offer ongoing support after the project is completed?

Yes, I provide post-project support. This includes minor adjustments and answering questions about your new website for up to 30 days after delivery. If there's a need for longer support, we can discuss a retainer.

Do you do Web Design?

Yes, while I’m not a professional designer, I can handle simple design needs, or I’m happy to work with your designer or recommend one. My strength is making sure your website not only looks good but works perfectly across all devices.
